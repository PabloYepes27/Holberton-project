Belmira wasteland (Antioquia-CO)

Here I am, over 3.300 meters above sea level after 5 hours walking, completly wet, with a temperature below 10 degrees but with a huge smile in my face because what my eyes were seeing. Belmira moore or also called "Páramo de Santa inés" is located in the North West of Antioquia between 2400 msnm and 3350 msnm. There you will find a special ecosystem with 4 species of plants that only live there, including Espeletia, commonly known as "Frailejones" which grow only one centimeter per year.
